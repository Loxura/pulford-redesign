Employment with PCLS Inc.
"Are you wanting to make a difference? Don't know where to go, then look no further. Pulford Community Living Services Inc., is a dynamic not-for-profit organization that provides many employment opportunities. It is essential that potential employees not only demonstrate a strong commitment to the values and beliefs identified in Pulford's mission, but are also dedicated to high standards of excellence and quality in providing supports to people with a developmental disability. In turn, Pulford is committed to working with its employees to ensure that they are knowledgeable and well equipped to carry out their responsibilities."

Our Employees Enjoy:
- Competitive Compensation
- Health and Dental Benefits
- Company Pension Plan
- New Employee Orientation
- On-going Training
- Opportunities for Advancement
- Flexible Work Schedules
- Paid Mileage
- Referral Bonus
- "Family" culture
