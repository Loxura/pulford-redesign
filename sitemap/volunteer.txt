How To Help
"Thank you for thinking about donating your time with us. To apply for a volunteer position, please fill out an application form."

Step 1: "Please download the application form and fill out digitally. CLICK HERE to download Online Application Form"
Step 2: "Please fill out your contact info in the form below"
Step 3: "Upload both your resume either in word or PDF format, and your filled out application form you downloaded and filled out in to appropriate upload button named 'Choose File'. We require both documents for employment."

Volunteering with PCLS Inc.
"When you sign on to volunteer with Pulford Community Living Services, you will be providing support(s) in a variety of activities and programs assisting people to lead a self-directed lives and to participate fully in their communities. Volunteering allows you to build your resume, learn new skills, keep busy, discover new strengths and talents, share special skills and have lots of fun."
