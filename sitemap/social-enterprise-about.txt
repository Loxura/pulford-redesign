About Social Enterprise
"Social enterprise is business that helps us grow. Using this model, we are able to create employment opportunities for the people we support, generate revenues to expand our services and programming, and help make Manitoba a better place to live and do business for everyone!"

"Pulford is proud to operate three social enterprises, which have provided over 50,000 hours of job training and employment for people with intellectual disabilities in 2017."
