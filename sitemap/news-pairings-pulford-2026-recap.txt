=== ARTICLE CONTENT ===
The fundraising event occurred on Friday, April 10, 2026, featuring a welcoming venue with natural light from expansive windows. The space encouraged organic guest circulation between conversation areas and food stations, creating "a dynamic rhythm that kept the evening lively and energized."

Event Purpose:
Attendees heard directly from individuals supported by the organization. This storytelling made tangible the fundraising objective: purchasing a wheelchair-accessible vehicle representing "access, independence, dignity, and opportunity."

Acknowledgments:
The organization expressed gratitude to donors, sponsors, vendors, and volunteers. A special note stated: "Your presence and enthusiasm are what transform a fundraising event into a true community celebration."
Individuals unable to attend could contribute by emailing payment@pulford.ca with "wheelchair-accessible vehicle" noted.

Future Plans:
The organization announced upcoming celebrations would mark "15 years of fundraising," encouraging continued community connection and impact expansion.
