Services

"Supports are provided to assist individuals with all the tasks of daily life. Everything from grocery shopping, and maintaining a household, to social occasions and celebrations!"

Service Categories:

Supported Independent Living
"Our Supported Independent Living services respect the diversity and individuality of each person. The staff help them to lead self-directed lives with person-centered supports chosen by the individual."
[Learn More]

Support for Families
"Outreach supports to families are provided to assist individuals outside the family home."
[Learn More]

Day Services
"Our Day Service is community-based and centered upon each person's wants and needs. We take into consideration their unique goals and abilities."
[Learn More]

Residential
"Each home is as unique as the people who live there. Pulford strives to ensure that each person feels safe in their own home, and that they have a sense of ownership of where they live."
