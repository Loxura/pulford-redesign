=== MAIN CONTENT ===
Mission Statement: "To Bring Community Together to Promote and Empower People to Live a Full Life."

Our Story:
Pulford Community Living Services Inc. is a not-for-profit organization established in November 1986 following the closure of a guest home. On December 1, 1986, three individuals moved into their own home on Fleet Avenue with Pulford's assistance.

Our Philosophy:
On October 4, 1996, Manitoba enacted The Adults Living with an Intellectual Disability Act (ALIDA), replacing the Vulnerable Persons Act. PCLS draws its philosophy from ALIDA to promote and protect rights of adults with intellectual disabilities. The act reflects ethical standards inherent in Pulford's policies since 1986.
