The Second Hand Store · Ste. Anne

Every purchase is a legacy.

The Store sells gently used clothing, furniture, housewares and décor at prices that make sense — and it's an employment initiative first. Every dollar of revenue goes into our Endowment Fund at The Winnipeg Foundation, so what you buy today keeps giving for good.

We gratefully accept donations of gently used merchandise during open hours. We can't take mattresses and pillows, undergarments, toiletries or open liquids, protective sports equipment, electronics, damaged items, or games with missing pieces.
