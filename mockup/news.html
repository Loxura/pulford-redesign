An evening built by the whole community

Pairings with Pulford 2026, recapped. On Friday, April 10, a room full of daylight and good food kept the evening lively — but the heart of the night was a story told by the people we support. It made the goal tangible: a new wheelchair-accessible vehicle. Not just a van — access, independence, dignity and opportunity.

Couldn't make it? You can still help — email payment@pulford.ca and note "wheelchair-accessible vehicle". Next year we celebrate 15 years of fundraising together.
