# Pulford Pitch — 10-Minute Script

*(Italics = stage directions. Bold = beats to hit word-for-word if nerves kick in.)*

---

## 1 · WARM OPEN — 1 min

Hi, I'm Ayub — I design and build websites, and I've spent the last while getting to know Pulford's.

**"Before I show you anything, I'd love to hear from you first — because what we made only makes sense in light of how you see yourselves."**

---

## 2 · THEIR TURN — 3 min · *Listen. Write down their EXACT words.*

**Q1.** "When you look at your current website — how does it make you feel? Does it feel like Pulford to you?"
*(If shy: "When did the website last feel like part of Pulford's work?")*

**Q2.** "Your logo — the child walking through the open door. What does that mean to you?"
*(Whatever they say becomes your demo narration. Underline it.)*

**Q3.** "When a parent looking for support, or someone who wants to give, lands on the new site — what do you want them to feel in the first five seconds?"

⏰ *Pivot line if time runs:* "I could listen to this all day — and I want to make sure I show you something, because it speaks to exactly what you just said."

---

## 3 · THE REVEAL — 4 min · *Open mockup A. Narrate in THEIR words.*

**"Can I show you what we made while sitting with exactly that?"**

- **Hero doorway:** "You said the door means ______. We didn't want that story living only in the corner of the header — so we let it become the whole site."
- **Four doorway cards** *(hover slowly — let the doors swing)*: "Everyone who comes to you walks through a door — a family, a job seeker, a donor, a partner. So we gave each of them one."
- **Daylight / Dusk toggle:** "Comfortable for every eye, day or night."
- **One inside page** *(Services)*: "Your real photos, your real words, plain language. Your logo, untouched, right where it's always been. **We didn't redesign Pulford — we tried to listen to it.**"
- **CMS** *(type something, hit Publish)*: "And it stays yours. Your team writes and publishes here — no technician, no fees, in plain words."

---

## 4 · THEIR CHOICE — 1 min · *Open mockup C briefly.*

"We also made a quieter, more formal version. **Which of these feels more like you?**"

*(If asked your lean: "Honestly, the warm one — because of what you said about ______.")*

*(If they push back on anything: don't defend. "That's exactly the kind of thing we'd shape together." Write it down visibly.)*

---

## 5 · CLOSE — 1 min

**"We'd love to build this with you.** The next step is simple — we sit down together, walk through your pages and your words, and shape it into the real thing. Can we book that?"

*…then questions, relaxed. Questions are buying signals.*

---

### Pocket answers
- **Cost / timeline:** *(decide your number & weeks before walking in — say it in the room, not in a PDF later)*
- **Who maintains it?** "You do — that editor you saw. We stay on call."
- **Donations?** "Through CanadaHelps — trusted, receipts automatic, no payment risk on you."
- **Board page names?** "Placeholders, clearly labeled — we'd put your real people in."
