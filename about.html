About Pulford

Forty years of open doors.

Pulford Community Living Services is a Manitoba non-profit, founded in 1986, supporting adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities to live the lives they choose.

Our story

It started with a door.

Our logo is a child pushing open a door onto a community — houses, trees, neighbours. It isn't decoration; it's the whole idea. For almost four decades, our job has been to hold that door open: to a home of your own, to meaningful work, to friendship, and to a seat at every table in Manitoba life.

From our beginnings in Winnipeg we've grown into three regions — Winnipeg, the Interlake at Lundar, and Eastman at Ste. Anne — supporting people through day services, residential homes, family support, and supported independent living.

We are a registered charity governed by a volunteer board, and we measure our work the only way that matters: in full lives, lived in full colour.
