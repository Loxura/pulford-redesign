Get Involved · Work · Volunteer · Give

Three ways to open a door.

Every door at Pulford is opened by a person — a support professional, a volunteer, a donor. Choose the way that fits your life.

Careers

Work that means something

Direct Support Professionals are the heart of Pulford. No experience in the field? We train. What we can't train is care — bring that, and we'll teach the rest.

  • Paid training and mentorship
  • Full-time, part-time and casual roles
  • Winnipeg, Interlake and Eastman locations
Ask about openings
Volunteer

Give an afternoon, gain a community

Share a skill, join an outing, help at an event — volunteers widen the circle around the people we support. A few hours a month genuinely changes someone's week.

  • Program and event volunteering
  • Skill-sharing — music, cooking, gardening
  • Board and committee service

Give

A gift here opens a real door.

Donations fund the things public dollars don't reach — recreation, transportation, home comforts, and the small dignities that make a house a home. Every dollar stays in Manitoba.
